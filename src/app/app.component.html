Who We Are

The artist Salvador Dali once said, “Those who do not want to imitate anything, produce nothing.” As an artist herself, Theresa Royster watched her father start, build, and lead a successful business in central Pennsylvania. It was through his entrepreneurial spirit and leadership that steered Theresa on her path as a successful business woman, allowing her to creatively produce results during her 30+ year career with State Farm Insurance Company in critical leadership positions throughout dynamic and challenging markets across the country.

Theresa’s creativity stems from her enjoyment of art and has allowed her to think outside of the box when it came to business. As a member of State Farm’s leadership team, and as an agent prior to that, she has always believed in the importance of recruiting, selection and development or entrepreneurs and leaders. Theresa also has experience in talent strategy and played an important role in succession planning and talent management reporting to the C-suite. She participated in the creative innovation and long- term strategy and scenario planning with a focus on the implications for recruiting, talent development and leadership preparation.

The passion for developing people and helping them succeed is her drive for continuing to consult and coach. Theresa’s goals are to help others perform at their best and reach their goals. As Vincent van Gogh said, “I would rather die of passion than of boredom.”

A certified coach and entrepreneur, she is a graduate of Loyola College in Baltimore, MD and has earned various insurance designations. She participated and completed a number of Executive Development programs in U.S, Asia and Europe. She was honored to be voted one of Georgia’s Most Powerful and Influential Women in 2004.

Theresa and her husband, Steve reside in Saint Simons Island, GA. They have two children, Amy and Michael and two grandsons, Gabriel and Noah for whom she has filled their rooms with her artwork.

Theresa is known for her business savvy, achieving or exceeding business results. Coupled with creativity and strategic thinking, she’s a problem solver and critical thinker able to understand mega-trends and their implications to organizations, strategy and leaders.

Branded as a turn-around market leader, developer of high performing teams and coach, who motivates individuals to perform at their best, Rendi co-created TBR Coaching to share her passion of coaching individuals to their full potential.

Rendi was raised in a small rural community on a farm where hard work was the norm and competing in athletics taught her many valuable lessons’ she would mine for years to come. She realized her dream to become a teacher/coach which ultimately paved a path for a future outside the classroom.

After teaching a few years, Rendi was approached to start up a State Farm Agency from “scratch”—meaning it came with zero clients— and was soon recognized as a top-performing agent in that market. Yet, that calling to teach and coach never went away. Recognized for her drive and influence, Rendi was offered a leadership position that enabled her to do what she loved: coaching individuals and teams to be at their best, while -growing revenue and market share.

Her accomplishments range from leading the performance turn-around of major metropolitan markets in multiple states. She attributes her success to the identification and development of talent; creating and leading high performing teams; and coaching individuals to perform at their best. She is known for creating a healthy and sustainable culture of performance that attracts followers. Understanding and leveraging the mental game with herself and others is the foundation of her leadership and coaching approach.

Rendi is uniquely positioned to leverage 30+ plus years personal and team success to help YOU and YOUR TEAMS. Her passion for identifying talent, bringing out the best in others, and driving results will inspire you to do the same.

Rendi is a certified coach and business owner. She is a graduate of the University of Tulsa in Tulsa, Oklahoma. She is passionate about personal growth and development and has continued her learning by achieving multiple insurance designations along with years of executive development experiences.

Rendi and her husband Randy, reside in Dallas, Texas. They have two children, Erin and Whitney and their first grandson Carter.
